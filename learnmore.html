So...how Pushnews works?


We at Pushnews curate news from all the top news sources. We remove the clutter and select the most important news of the time. Then, we summarize the news small enough to fit your notification centre. And then, as the name suggests, we push the news to you in real time (5 times a day currently) so you can stay updated without opening a news app ever.


But I get news from all my news apps through push notification......


No, you get headlines (sometimes incomplete) that call you back to the app to read the news. Pushnews pushes the summarized news to you rather than a headline. Remember, Headline â‰  News.


What happens when I click on a push notification? (Features)


What happens? Well, we call it magic. When you click on a push notification the news loads with the following :

-> Dig Deeper - Want to learn more about the news? You can do that and learn about the news from different news sources.

-> What's this about? - Ever read about some news and thought what's this about? Why is this news trending? You can learn what's the news about with direct quotations from Wikipedia.

Did you notice?

In other news app, the news loads after the app. Here the news loads first to save your time. You are welcome :)
